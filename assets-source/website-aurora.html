About the band

Prog from Katwijk

The Aurora Project was founded in 1998 by a group of friends who met through their shared love of Magic: The Gathering and progressive rock & metal. Inspired by Rush, Pink Floyd and The Gathering, the band builds expansive concept albums through intensive jam sessions.

After the sudden passing of guitarist and lyricist Marc Vooijs in 2014, the band wove his ideas and lyrics into World of Grey. Now they return with EVOS12: part one of an ambitious two-part concept.
